Programmes you can run in your own town.

Linguist makes the pack. You run the room. We don't franchise, we don't charge, we don't take a cut.

Some kinds of AI learning don't happen on a website. They happen in a library meeting room, with tea and a bun, between neighbours. These are the programmes we've designed for that — free to use, free to adapt, made to be hosted by someone in town who's not us.
