Tea & Tech

Pop in if you don't want to wait til the weekend.

A free Tuesday morning for people over 65 who'd like to get the hang of AI — without feeling like they're back in school.

Tea, scones, and a patient hand. 90 minutes. By the end, you can talk to Claude on your own phone. Run by your local library, or anyone in town who fancies hosting one.

    Reading old parish records, organising what you know, writing a one-page family story a grandchild would actually read.
