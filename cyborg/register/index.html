Sign up for the assessment

A 7-day analyst-at-Veralux simulation. Fill in your details below and we'll email you a single link to launch the workspace — no install, no token to remember. Runs in your browser.

What happens next

  1. You submit the form.
  2. We email you a launch link (usually within a minute).
  3. Click the link → your workspace boots in ~30 seconds → you start working.
  4. You have 7 days to fit in 8 hours of active work. Pause the workspace when you step away — your work-time clock stops with it.
  5. Submit when you're done; you'll get a written feedback report.

Treat it like a real first day: a couple of focused sittings beats grinding through it in one go. Quality of judgement, not hours logged.

One thing to bring

Inside the workspace, the AI assistant (Claude Code) is pre-installed. The first time you run it, it'll ask you to sign in — any free Anthropic account works, no payment, no API key required. It takes about 30 seconds.

The same sign-in also enables the in-app stakeholder DMs (Slack-style messaging with team members in the simulation). If you'd rather not sign in, you can paste an Anthropic API key in the Messages settings panel instead — keys are scoped to this session and never logged.
